Producer Consumer

The Producer-Consumer problem is a classical multi-process synchronization problem, that is we are trying to achieve synchronization between more than one processes.

Dining Philosophers

The dining philosopher's problem is the classical problem of synchronization which says that Five philosophers are sitting around a circular table and their job is to think and eat alternatively.

Reader Writer

The readers-writers problem is a classical problem of process synchronization, it relates to a data set such as a file that is shared between more than one process at a time.
